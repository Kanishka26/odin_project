Pani Puri

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Semolina
  • 1/2 cup Wheat Flour
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 1/2 cup Salt
  • 1/2 cup Oil
  • 1/2 cup Potatoes
  • 1/2 cup Onions
  • 1/2 cup Chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup Tamarind Chutney
  • 1/2 cup Mint Chutney
  • 1/2 cup Chaat Masala
  • 1/2 cup Chilli Powder
  • 1/2 cup Salt
  • 1/2 cup Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 cup Coriander Leaves

Instructions:

  1. Mix semolina, wheat flour, water, salt, and oil to make a dough.
  2. Roll out small puris from the dough.
  3. Fry the puris in oil.
  4. Make a hole in the puris
  5. Fill the puris with potatoes, onions, and chickpeas.
  6. Add tamarind chutney, mint chutney, chaat masala, chilli powder, and salt.
  7. Add lemon juice and coriander leaves.
  8. Fill the puris with water.
  9. Serve the pani puri.
