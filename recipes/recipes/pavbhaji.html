Pav Bhaji

Ingredients:

  • 2 Potatoes
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Capsicum
  • 1 cup Peas
  • 1/2 cup Cauliflower
  • 1/2 cup Carrot
  • 1/2 cup French Beans
  • 1/2 cup Beetroot
  • 1/2 cup Cabbage
  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 1/2 cup Pav Bhaji Masala
  • 1/2 cup Chilli Powder
  • 1/2 cup Salt
  • 1/2 cup Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 cup Coriander Leaves
  • Pav Buns

Instructions:

  1. Boil all the vegetables in a pressure cooker.
  2. Mash the vegetables and keep aside.
  3. Heat butter in a pan and add chopped onions.
  4. Once the onions turn golden brown, add chopped tomatoes.
  5. Once the tomatoes are cooked, add the mashed vegetables.
  6. Add pav bhaji masala, chilli powder, and salt.
  7. Let it cook for 10 minutes.
  8. Add lemon juice and coriander leaves.
  9. Toast the pav buns with butter.
  10. Serve the pav bhaji with toasted pav buns.
