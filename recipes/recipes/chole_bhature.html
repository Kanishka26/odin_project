Chole Bhature

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Chickpeas
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Capsicum
  • 1 cup Peas
  • 1/2 cup Cauliflower
  • 1/2 cup Carrot
  • 1/2 cup French Beans
  • 1/2 cup Beetroot
  • 1/2 cup Cabbage
  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 1/2 cup Chole Masala
  • 1/2 cup Chilli Powder
  • 1/2 cup Salt
  • 1/2 cup Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 cup Coriander Leaves
  • Bhature

Instructions:

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight.
  2. Boil the chickpeas in a pressure cooker.
  3. Heat butter in a pan and add chopped onions.
  4. Once the onions turn golden brown, add chopped tomatoes.
  5. Once the tomatoes are cooked, add the boiled chickpeas.
  6. Add chole masala, chilli powder, and salt.
  7. Let it cook for 10 minutes.
  8. Add lemon juice and coriander leaves.
  9. Deep fry the bhature.
  10. Serve the chole with bhature.
